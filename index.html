Welcome to my website. This is just a small site detailing vintage cars from the 1960s and 1970s. Photographs, information and videos of the cars in action can be found. Feel free to contact me also with any feedback or comments. I've chosen these cars as I love cars from the past, especially from a design perspective. I've been fortunate enough to experience all of these cars first hand too and I will talk about my thoughts and experiences with all of them. For me, car design peaked in the 1960s and 1970s and very few cars compare to the beauty of vintage classics.
